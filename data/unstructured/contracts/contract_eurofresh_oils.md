# SUPPLIER AGREEMENT CONTRACT

**Contract ID:** CNT-2024-002  
**Date:** January 15, 2024  
**Parties:** Golden Harvest Foods (Buyer) and EuroFresh Oils GmbH (Supplier)

## 1. PRODUCT SPECIFICATIONS

**Product:** Organic Canola Oil (Cold-Pressed)  
**Quality Standard:** ISO 22000, FSSC 22000, EU Organic Certified, Non-GMO  
**Packaging:** 500ml glass bottles, 1L PET bottles, or bulk IBC containers  
**Shelf Life:** Minimum 12 months from production date

## 2. PRICING AND PAYMENT TERMS

- **Base Price:** EUR 2.85 per kg (EXW Germany) ≈ USD 3.10 per kg
- **Volume Discount:** 
  - 10,000+ kg: 3% discount
  - 25,000+ kg: 6% discount
  - 50,000+ kg: 10% discount
- **Payment Terms:** Net 45 days from invoice date
- **Currency:** EUR or USD (buyer's choice)
- **Price Protection:** Fixed pricing for 6 months, then quarterly review

## 3. DELIVERY TERMS

- **Lead Time:** 14 days from confirmed purchase order
- **Delivery Schedule:** Bi-weekly shipments
- **Minimum Order Quantity:** 10,000 kg
- **Incoterms:** DDP to buyer's warehouse (Germany, France, Netherlands)
- **Delivery Reliability Target:** 98% on-time delivery

## 4. QUALITY ASSURANCE

- **Organic Certification:** EU Organic Regulation (EC) No 834/2007 compliant
- **Non-GMO Verification:** Third-party verified, IP (Identity Preserved) handling
- **Certificate of Analysis (CoA):** Required with each shipment
- **Quality Parameters:**
  - Peroxide Value: < 5 meq/kg (cold-pressed standard)
  - Free Fatty Acids: < 0.5% (as oleic acid)
  - Moisture & Volatiles: < 0.05%
  - Omega-3 Content: Minimum 9%
  - Color: Golden yellow, characteristic of cold-pressed canola
- **Testing:** Independent lab testing for pesticide residues, heavy metals

## 5. SUSTAINABILITY & TRACEABILITY

- **Carbon Footprint:** Annual carbon footprint reporting required
- **Sustainable Farming:** Regenerative agriculture practices preferred
- **Traceability:** Farm-to-bottle traceability with blockchain verification
- **Packaging:** Recyclable or biodegradable packaging materials only
- **Sustainability Score Target:** Minimum 8.5/10

## 6. PREMIUM QUALITY REQUIREMENTS

- **Cold-Pressed Only:** No heat or chemical extraction permitted
- **First Press:** Only first-press oil acceptable
- **Storage Conditions:** Nitrogen blanketing during storage and transport
- **Light Protection:** Amber glass or UV-protective packaging required
- **Taste Profile:** Mild, nutty flavor; no rancidity or off-flavors

## 7. RISK AND LIABILITY

- **Product Liability Insurance:** Supplier maintains EUR 10M coverage
- **Recall Insurance:** EUR 2M recall insurance required
- **Quality Failure:** Full refund + 20% penalty for premium product failures
- **Late Delivery Penalties:** 1% per day, max 10% (higher standard for premium)

## 8. COMPLIANCE & CERTIFICATIONS

- **Organic Certification:** Annual renewal and audit compliance
- **FSSC 22000:** Maintain certification throughout contract
- **Allergen Control:** Dedicated allergen-free production lines
- **Regulatory Compliance:** EU, FDA, and local food safety regulations

## 9. CONTRACT DURATION

- **Term:** 24 months (January 15, 2024 - January 14, 2026)
- **Renewal:** Negotiation required 120 days before expiry
- **Termination:** 90 days written notice required

## 10. INNOVATION & COLLABORATION

- **New Product Development:** Joint R&D for new organic oil blends
- **Market Intelligence:** Quarterly market trend sharing sessions
- **Continuous Improvement:** Annual supplier performance review
