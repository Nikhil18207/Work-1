# SUPPLIER AGREEMENT CONTRACT

**Contract ID:** CNT-2024-001  
**Date:** January 1, 2024  
**Parties:** FreshMart Retail Chain (Buyer) and Golden Sun Oils Ltd (Supplier)

## 1. PRODUCT SPECIFICATIONS

**Product:** Sunflower Oil (Refined)  
**Quality Standard:** ISO 22000, RSPO Certified, Halal Certified  
**Packaging:** 1L PET bottles, 5L jerry cans, or bulk tankers  
**Shelf Life:** Minimum 18 months from production date

## 2. PRICING AND PAYMENT TERMS

- **Base Price:** USD 2.45 per kg (FOB Malaysia)
- **Volume Discount:** 
  - 50,000+ kg: 2% discount
  - 100,000+ kg: 5% discount
  - 200,000+ kg: 8% discount
- **Payment Terms:** Net 60 days from invoice date
- **Currency:** USD
- **Price Adjustment:** Quarterly review based on global market indices

## 3. DELIVERY TERMS

- **Lead Time:** 21 days from confirmed purchase order
- **Delivery Schedule:** Weekly shipments
- **Minimum Order Quantity:** 5,000 kg
- **Incoterms:** FOB Port Klang, Malaysia
- **Delivery Reliability Target:** 95% on-time delivery

## 4. QUALITY ASSURANCE

- **Inspection:** Pre-shipment inspection mandatory
- **Certificate of Analysis (CoA):** Required with each shipment
- **Quality Parameters:**
  - Peroxide Value: < 10 meq/kg
  - Free Fatty Acids: < 0.1%
  - Moisture: < 0.1%
  - Color: Light yellow, clear
- **Rejection Criteria:** Any deviation from agreed specifications allows buyer to reject shipment

## 5. SUSTAINABILITY REQUIREMENTS

- **RSPO Certification:** Mandatory for all palm-based products
- **Traceability:** Full supply chain traceability to plantation level
- **Environmental Compliance:** ISO 14001 compliance required
- **Ethical Sourcing:** No child labor, fair wages, safe working conditions

## 6. RISK AND LIABILITY

- **Product Liability Insurance:** Supplier maintains USD 5M coverage
- **Force Majeure:** Standard force majeure clauses apply
- **Penalties for Late Delivery:** 0.5% of order value per day, max 5%
- **Quality Failure Penalties:** Full refund + replacement cost + 10% penalty

## 7. CONTRACT DURATION

- **Term:** 12 months (January 1, 2024 - December 31, 2024)
- **Renewal:** Automatic renewal unless 90 days notice given
- **Termination:** Either party may terminate with 60 days written notice

## 8. COMPLIANCE

- **Food Safety:** Compliance with FDA, EU, and local food safety regulations
- **Certifications:** Maintain all certifications throughout contract period
- **Audits:** Buyer reserves right to conduct supplier audits with 30 days notice
