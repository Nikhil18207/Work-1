## 1. Strategic Sourcing Policy {#strategic-sourcing}

### 1.1 Category Management
- **Objective:** Optimize spend across all procurement categories
- **Approach:** Segment categories by strategic importance and market complexity
- **Categories:**
  - **Strategic:** High value, high risk (e.g., raw materials, critical components)
  - **Leverage:** High value, low risk (e.g., commodities with multiple suppliers)
  - **Bottleneck:** Low value, high risk (e.g., specialized services)
  - **Non-Critical:** Low value, low risk (e.g., office supplies)

### 1.2 Sourcing Strategies by Category
- **Strategic Categories:** Long-term partnerships, joint development, risk sharing
- **Leverage Categories:** Competitive bidding, volume consolidation, price negotiation
- **Bottleneck Categories:** Supplier development, backup qualification, inventory buffering
- **Non-Critical Categories:** E-procurement, automated ordering, supplier consolidation

### 1.3 Global vs Local Sourcing
- **Global Sourcing:** When cost savings >20%, quality superior, or local unavailable
- **Local Sourcing:** When lead time critical, regulatory requirements, or supporting local economy
- **Hybrid Approach:** Balance cost, risk, and responsiveness

## 2. Supplier Selection & Qualification {#supplier-selection}

### 2.1 Supplier Evaluation Criteria

#### Financial Stability (20% weight)
- Debt-to-equity ratio <2.5
- Current ratio >1.5
- 3+ years of positive cash flow
- Credit rating minimum: B+

#### Quality Capability (25% weight)
- ISO 9001 certification (mandatory)
- Quality rejection rate <5%
- Six Sigma or equivalent quality program
- Traceability systems in place

#### Delivery Performance (20% weight)
- On-time delivery rate >85%
- Lead time competitiveness
- Flexibility to handle demand variability
- Geographic proximity (where relevant)

#### Cost Competitiveness (15% weight)
- Pricing within 10% of market benchmark
- Total cost of ownership (TCO) analysis
- Payment terms flexibility
- Currency stability

#### Sustainability & ESG (10% weight)
- ESG score >60 (mandatory minimum)
- Environmental certifications (ISO 14001, RSPO, etc.)
- Labor practices compliance (SA8000, Fair Trade)
- Carbon footprint disclosure

#### Innovation & Technology (10% weight)
- R&D investment >3% of revenue
- Digital capabilities (EDI, API integration)
- Continuous improvement programs
- Industry thought leadership

### 2.2 Supplier Qualification Process
1. **Pre-Qualification:** RFI (Request for Information) screening
2. **Detailed Assessment:** Site visits, capability audits, reference checks
3. **Trial Orders:** Small-scale testing of quality and delivery
4. **Full Qualification:** Approved vendor list (AVL) addition
5. **Ongoing Monitoring:** Quarterly performance reviews

### 2.3 Supplier Segmentation
- **Strategic Partners:** Top 10% of spend, long-term contracts, joint innovation
- **Preferred Suppliers:** 20-30% of spend, competitive pricing, reliable delivery
- **Approved Suppliers:** Qualified but not preferred, used for competition
- **Conditional Suppliers:** Under performance improvement plans
- **Blacklisted:** Failed compliance, quality, or ethical standards

## 3. Risk Management Framework {#risk-management}

### 3.1 Supply Chain Risk Categories

#### Geographic Concentration Risk (Rule R001)
- **Threshold:** >40% spend in single region
- **Mitigation:** Diversify across 3-4 regions minimum
- **Monitoring:** Monthly regional spend analysis

#### Single Supplier Dependency (Rule R003)
- **Threshold:** >60% spend with single supplier
- **Mitigation:** Qualify 2-3 suppliers per critical category
- **Monitoring:** Supplier concentration index

#### Geopolitical Risk (Rule R024)
- **Assessment:** Country risk ratings (Moody's, S&P)
- **High-Risk Countries:** Political instability, trade restrictions, sanctions
- **Mitigation:** Limit exposure to <40% in high-risk regions

#### Financial Risk (Rule R010)
- **Monitoring:** Supplier financial health quarterly
- **Red Flags:** Debt-to-equity >2.5, negative cash flow, credit downgrades
- **Action:** Secure inventory, qualify backups, request guarantees

#### Cybersecurity Risk (Rule R025)
- **Minimum:** B-rating from cybersecurity assessment
- **Requirements:** Data encryption, access controls, incident response plan
- **Audits:** Annual cybersecurity audits for critical suppliers

### 3.2 Business Continuity Planning

#### Backup Supplier Requirements (Rule R029)
- **Critical Categories:** Minimum 1 qualified backup supplier
- **Strategic Categories:** Minimum 2 qualified backup suppliers
- **Testing:** Annual backup supplier activation drills

#### Safety Stock Policies
- **High-Risk Suppliers:** 90 days safety stock
- **Medium-Risk Suppliers:** 60 days safety stock
- **Low-Risk Suppliers:** 30 days safety stock

#### Disaster Recovery
- **Supplier Mapping:** Identify all tier-1 and tier-2 suppliers
- **Risk Scenarios:** Natural disasters, pandemics, cyberattacks, geopolitical events
- **Response Plans:** Pre-approved alternative suppliers, emergency procurement authority

## 4. Cost Management & Optimization {#cost-management}

### 4.1 Total Cost of Ownership (TCO)
**Components:**
- Purchase price (60-70%)
- Transportation & logistics (10-15%)
- Quality costs (defects, returns, rework) (5-10%)
- Inventory carrying costs (5-10%)
- Administrative costs (3-5%)
- Risk costs (supply disruptions, currency, etc.) (2-5%)

### 4.2 Cost Reduction Strategies

#### Volume Consolidation (Rule R002, R033)
- **Tail Spend:** Consolidate suppliers from 20 to 5 (10-20% savings)
- **Volume Discounts:** Negotiate tiered pricing based on volume
- **Demand Aggregation:** Combine requirements across business units

#### Price Benchmarking (Rule R019)
- **Threshold:** Price should not exceed market benchmark by >10%
- **Sources:** Industry reports, competitor intelligence, market indices
- **Action:** Renegotiate or switch suppliers if deviation exceeds threshold

#### Payment Terms Optimization (Rule R009)
- **Target:** 45-60 days payment terms
- **Cash Flow Benefit:** Improves working capital by 15-20%
- **Negotiation:** Offer volume commitments for extended terms

#### Currency Hedging (Rule R014)
- **Threshold:** >50% spend in volatile foreign currency
- **Instruments:** Forward contracts, options, natural hedging
- **Review:** Quarterly currency exposure analysis

### 4.3 Should-Cost Modeling
- **Bottom-Up Costing:** Material, labor, overhead, margin analysis
- **Benchmarking:** Compare against industry standards
- **Negotiation:** Use should-cost as basis for price discussions

## 5. Quality Assurance Standards {#quality-assurance}

### 5.1 Quality Requirements

#### Mandatory Certifications
- ISO 9001 (Quality Management)
- HACCP (Food Safety) - for food categories
- ISO 22000 (Food Safety Management) - for food categories
- GMP (Good Manufacturing Practices)

#### Quality Metrics (Rule R007)
- **Rejection Rate:** <5% (mandatory)
- **First Pass Yield:** >95%
- **Customer Complaints:** <1 per 10,000 units
- **Defect Rate:** <100 PPM (parts per million)

### 5.2 Quality Control Process
1. **Incoming Inspection:** Sample testing of all shipments
2. **In-Process Monitoring:** Supplier process audits
3. **Final Inspection:** Pre-shipment verification
4. **Continuous Improvement:** Root cause analysis, corrective actions

### 5.3 Supplier Quality Development
- **Capability Assessments:** Annual supplier audits
- **Training Programs:** Quality management training for suppliers
- **Collaborative Improvement:** Joint quality improvement projects
- **Recognition:** Supplier quality awards for top performers

## 6. Sustainability & ESG Requirements {#sustainability}

### 6.1 Environmental Standards

#### Carbon Footprint (Rule R031)
- **Target:** <1000 kg CO2 per unit
- **Measurement:** Scope 1, 2, and 3 emissions
- **Reduction:** 5% year-over-year improvement
- **Reporting:** Annual carbon footprint disclosure

#### Certifications (Rule R026)
- **ISO 14001:** Environmental Management System
- **RSPO:** Roundtable on Sustainable Palm Oil (for palm oil)
- **FSC/PEFC:** Forest Stewardship Council (for paper/wood)
- **Rainforest Alliance:** For agricultural commodities

#### Waste Reduction
- **Target:** Zero waste to landfill by 2030
- **Circular Economy:** Promote recycling, reuse, remanufacturing
- **Packaging:** Minimize packaging, use recyclable materials

### 6.2 Social Responsibility

#### Labor Standards (Rule R032)
- **SA8000:** Social Accountability certification
- **Fair Trade:** For applicable commodities
- **No Child Labor:** Zero tolerance policy
- **Fair Wages:** Living wage minimum
- **Safe Working Conditions:** OSHA compliance or equivalent

#### Diversity & Inclusion (Rule R015)
- **Target:** 15% spend with diverse suppliers
- **Categories:** Minority-owned, women-owned, veteran-owned, LGBTQ+-owned
- **Support:** Supplier development programs for diverse businesses

### 6.3 Governance & Ethics (Rule R032)

#### Anti-Corruption
- **Zero Tolerance:** No bribes, kickbacks, or facilitation payments
- **Transparency:** Disclosure of all conflicts of interest
- **Compliance:** FCPA, UK Bribery Act, local anti-corruption laws

#### Data Privacy
- **GDPR Compliance:** For EU data processing
- **Data Security:** Encryption, access controls, breach notification
- **Confidentiality:** NDA requirements for all suppliers

## 7. Contract Management {#contract-management}

### 7.1 Contract Types

#### Long-Term Agreements (3-5 years)
- **Use:** Strategic categories, high-value spend
- **Benefits:** Price stability, capacity commitment, joint innovation
- **Requirements:** Performance guarantees, exit clauses

#### Master Service Agreements (MSAs)
- **Use:** Services, recurring purchases
- **Benefits:** Standardized terms, simplified ordering
- **Structure:** Framework agreement + individual work orders

#### Spot Purchases
- **Use:** One-time needs, non-critical items
- **Benefits:** Flexibility, competitive pricing
- **Process:** Simplified approval, standard PO terms

### 7.2 Contract Terms & Conditions

#### Pricing Mechanisms
- **Fixed Price:** For stable commodities
- **CPI-Indexed:** For long-term contracts (Rule R028)
- **Cost-Plus:** For development projects
- **Market-Based:** For volatile commodities

#### Performance Guarantees
- **On-Time Delivery:** >85% (Rule R008)
- **Quality:** <5% rejection rate (Rule R007)
- **Responsiveness:** <48 hours response time (Rule R020)
- **Penalties:** Liquidated damages for non-performance

#### Contract Lifecycle (Rule R004)
- **Renewal Trigger:** 90 days before expiry
- **Renegotiation:** Annual price reviews
- **Termination:** 30-60 days notice period
- **Transition:** Smooth handover to new supplier

### 7.3 Contract Compliance (Rule R021)
- **Monitoring:** Monthly compliance tracking
- **Threshold:** >90% compliance rate required
- **Enforcement:** Penalties for non-compliance
- **Escalation:** Termination for repeated violations

## 8. Compliance & Regulatory {#compliance}

### 8.1 Regulatory Requirements

#### Local Content (Rule R017)
- **Threshold:** 30% minimum local content (varies by country)
- **Verification:** Certificates of origin, local supplier documentation
- **Penalties:** Fines, contract cancellation for non-compliance

#### Import/Export Regulations
- **Customs Compliance:** Proper classification, valuation, documentation
- **Trade Agreements:** Leverage FTAs for duty savings
- **Sanctions:** Screen all suppliers against OFAC, UN, EU sanctions lists

#### Industry-Specific Regulations
- **Food Safety:** FDA, EFSA, local food safety authorities
- **Pharmaceuticals:** GMP, FDA approval
- **Chemicals:** REACH, RoHS compliance
- **Electronics:** CE marking, FCC certification

### 8.2 Audit & Compliance

#### Supplier Audits (Rule R027)
- **Frequency:** High-risk suppliers every 12 months
- **Scope:** Quality, environmental, social, financial
- **Certification:** Third-party audit reports accepted

#### Internal Audits
- **Frequency:** Annual procurement process audits
- **Scope:** Policy compliance, contract adherence, spend analysis
- **Reporting:** Findings to audit committee

## 9. Supplier Relationship Management {#supplier-relationship}

### 9.1 Performance Management (Rule R035)

#### Scorecard Metrics (0-100 scale)
- **Quality:** 30% weight (rejection rate, complaints)
- **Delivery:** 25% weight (on-time, lead time)
- **Cost:** 20% weight (price competitiveness, TCO)
- **Responsiveness:** 10% weight (communication, issue resolution)
- **Innovation:** 10% weight (new ideas, process improvements)
- **Sustainability:** 5% weight (ESG score, certifications)

**Threshold:** >75 required (Rule R035)

#### Performance Reviews
- **Frequency:** Quarterly business reviews
- **Participants:** Procurement, quality, operations, supplier executives
- **Agenda:** Performance metrics, improvement plans, future opportunities

### 9.2 Supplier Development
- **Training:** Quality management, lean manufacturing, sustainability
- **Capability Building:** Technology transfer, process optimization
- **Financial Support:** Payment term flexibility, advance payments for capex
- **Recognition:** Supplier awards, preferred status, increased business

### 9.3 Relationship Tiers
- **Strategic Partners:** Executive sponsorship, joint business planning, innovation collaboration
- **Preferred Suppliers:** Regular business reviews, performance incentives
- **Transactional:** Standard terms, competitive bidding

## 10. Business Continuity {#business-continuity}

### 10.1 Risk Scenarios
- **Natural Disasters:** Earthquakes, floods, hurricanes
- **Pandemics:** COVID-19 type events
- **Geopolitical:** Wars, trade restrictions, sanctions
- **Cyberattacks:** Ransomware, data breaches
- **Supplier Bankruptcy:** Financial collapse

### 10.2 Mitigation Strategies
- **Dual Sourcing:** 2-3 qualified suppliers per critical category
- **Geographic Diversification:** Suppliers in different regions
- **Safety Stock:** 30-90 days based on risk level
- **Alternative Materials:** Qualified substitutes where possible
- **Insurance:** Business interruption, supply chain insurance

### 10.3 Crisis Response
- **Activation:** Procurement crisis team within 24 hours
- **Communication:** Stakeholder notification, status updates
- **Action:** Activate backup suppliers, expedite shipments, find alternatives
- **Recovery:** Post-crisis review, lessons learned, plan updates
