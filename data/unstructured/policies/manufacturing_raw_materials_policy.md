# Manufacturing & Raw Materials Procurement Policy

## 1. Overview
This policy governs the procurement of raw materials including steel, aluminum, copper, plastics, and other manufacturing inputs, as well as manufacturing equipment and machinery.

## 2. Supplier Selection Criteria

### 2.1 Mandatory Requirements
- **Certifications Required**:
  - ISO 9001 (Quality Management)
  - ISO 14001 (Environmental Management)
  - Industry-specific certifications (e.g., IATF 16949 for automotive)
- **Minimum Quality Rating**: 4.5/5.0
- **Delivery Reliability**: Minimum 92%
- **Financial Stability**: 5+ years in business, audited financials

### 2.2 Preferred Qualifications
- Vertical integration capabilities
- R&D and innovation capabilities
- Global supply chain presence
- Sustainability certifications (ISO 50001, CDP disclosure)
- Conflict-free minerals certification

## 3. Material Categories

### 3.1 Steel & Metals
- **Approved Suppliers**: ArcelorMittal, Tata Steel, Baosteel, Nucor
- **Quality Standards**:
  - Material test certificates (MTC) required
  - Tensile strength, yield strength specifications
  - Chemical composition analysis
  - Traceability to heat/batch number
- **Lead Time**: 21-30 days standard
- **Minimum Order**: 50-100 metric tons
- **Payment Terms**: Net 60 days

### 3.2 Aluminum
- **Approved Suppliers**: Alcoa, Rio Tinto, Norsk Hydro
- **Quality Standards**:
  - Alloy certification
  - Surface finish specifications
  - Dimensional tolerances
  - Recyclability documentation
- **Lead Time**: 18-25 days
- **Minimum Order**: 30-50 metric tons

### 3.3 Copper
- **Approved Suppliers**: Freeport-McMoRan, Codelco, Glencore
- **Quality Standards**:
  - Purity certification (99.9% minimum)
  - Conductivity testing
  - Impurity analysis
  - Conflict-free certification
- **Lead Time**: 20-28 days
- **Minimum Order**: 20-40 metric tons

### 3.4 Plastics & Polymers
- **Approved Suppliers**: BASF, Dow Chemical, SABIC, LyondellBasell
- **Quality Standards**:
  - Resin grade certification
  - Melt flow index (MFI) specifications
  - Color consistency
  - Recycled content percentage (if applicable)
- **Lead Time**: 14-21 days
- **Minimum Order**: 20-30 metric tons

## 4. Procurement Process

### 4.1 Approval Workflow
- **Under $100,000**: Procurement Manager approval
- **$100,000-$500,000**: VP Operations + Finance approval
- **$500,000-$2,000,000**: COO + CFO approval
- **Over $2,000,000**: Executive Committee approval

### 4.2 Vendor Evaluation
1. Quality and consistency (35%)
2. Pricing competitiveness (25%)
3. Delivery reliability (20%)
4. Technical support (10%)
5. Sustainability and ESG (10%)

### 4.3 Contract Terms
- **Contract Duration**: 12-36 months
- **Price Adjustment**: Quarterly based on commodity indices
- **Payment Terms**: Net 60 days
- **Force Majeure**: Clearly defined terms
- **Liability Caps**: Defined in contract

## 5. Quality Assurance

### 5.1 Incoming Inspection
- 100% inspection for critical materials
- Statistical sampling for standard materials (AQL 2.5)
- Third-party testing for first-time suppliers
- Certificate of Analysis (CoA) required for all shipments

### 5.2 Testing Requirements
- **Mechanical Testing**: Tensile, hardness, impact tests
- **Chemical Analysis**: Spectroscopy, wet chemistry
- **Dimensional Inspection**: CMM, calipers, micrometers
- **Non-Destructive Testing**: Ultrasonic, X-ray (as required)

### 5.3 Quality Metrics
- **Defect Rate**: Maximum 0.5% acceptable
- **First Pass Yield**: Minimum 98%
- **On-Time Delivery**: Minimum 95%
- **Documentation Accuracy**: 100%

## 6. Risk Management

### 6.1 Supply Chain Risks
- **Regional Concentration**: Maximum 50% from single region
- **Supplier Dependency**: Maximum 35% spend with single supplier
- **Commodity Price Risk**: Hedging strategy for volatile materials
- **Geopolitical Risk**: Diversification across stable regions

### 6.2 Inventory Management
- **Safety Stock**: 30-60 days for critical materials
- **Just-in-Time**: For non-critical, stable materials
- **Consignment Inventory**: For high-volume, low-value items
- **Vendor-Managed Inventory (VMI)**: For strategic suppliers

### 6.3 Business Continuity
- Dual sourcing for critical materials
- Alternative material qualifications
- Emergency supplier agreements
- Supply chain mapping and monitoring

## 7. Sustainability Requirements

### 7.1 Environmental Standards
- **Carbon Footprint**: Disclosure and reduction targets required
- **Recycled Content**: Minimum 25% for applicable materials
- **Waste Reduction**: Zero-waste-to-landfill preferred
- **Water Usage**: Reporting and reduction initiatives

### 7.2 Social Responsibility
- No child labor or forced labor
- Fair wages and working conditions
- Health and safety standards (ISO 45001)
- Community engagement programs

### 7.3 ESG Scoring
- Minimum sustainability score: 8.0/10
- Annual ESG audits for strategic suppliers
- Continuous improvement commitments
- Transparency in supply chain

## 8. Pricing & Cost Management

### 8.1 Pricing Mechanisms
- **Fixed Price**: For stable, predictable materials
- **Index-Based**: Tied to LME, Platts, or other indices
- **Cost-Plus**: For custom or specialized materials
- **Volume Discounts**: Tiered pricing based on annual volume

### 8.2 Cost Optimization
- Annual cost reduction targets (3-5%)
- Value engineering initiatives
- Material substitution analysis
- Total cost of ownership (TCO) evaluation

### 8.3 Price Benchmarking
- Quarterly market price reviews
- Competitive bidding for major contracts
- Should-cost modeling
- Industry price index tracking

## 9. Logistics & Delivery

### 9.1 Incoterms
- **Preferred**: DDP (Delivered Duty Paid) for imports
- **Alternative**: FOB, CIF with clear responsibility matrix
- **Insurance**: Supplier responsible until delivery

### 9.2 Packaging Requirements
- Returnable/reusable packaging preferred
- Protection against corrosion, damage
- Clear labeling with material specifications
- Palletization standards

### 9.3 Delivery Performance
- **On-Time Delivery**: 95% minimum
- **Complete Shipments**: No partial deliveries without approval
- **Advance Shipping Notice (ASN)**: 48 hours minimum
- **Expedited Delivery**: Available with premium (max 20%)

## 10. Technical Support

### 10.1 Material Specifications
- Technical data sheets (TDS) provided
- Material safety data sheets (MSDS/SDS)
- Processing guidelines
- Application support

### 10.2 Problem Resolution
- Dedicated technical support contact
- Root cause analysis for quality issues
- Corrective and preventive actions (CAPA)
- 8D problem-solving methodology

## 11. Compliance & Regulations

### 11.1 Regulatory Compliance
- RoHS, REACH compliance (for EU)
- Conflict minerals reporting (Dodd-Frank)
- California Prop 65 compliance
- Industry-specific regulations

### 11.2 Traceability
- Batch/lot traceability required
- Material genealogy documentation
- Recall procedures and capabilities
- Blockchain traceability (preferred)

## 12. Manufacturing Equipment Procurement

### 12.1 Equipment Categories
- **CNC Machines**: Siemens, Fanuc, Haas
- **Industrial Robots**: ABB, KUKA, Fanuc
- **Assembly Equipment**: Custom and standard
- **Testing Equipment**: Calibrated and certified

### 12.2 Equipment Requirements
- CE Mark compliance (for EU)
- Safety certifications (OSHA, ANSI)
- Maintenance and spare parts availability
- Training and documentation
- Warranty: Minimum 2 years

### 12.3 Total Cost of Ownership
- Purchase price
- Installation and commissioning
- Training costs
- Maintenance and spare parts
- Energy consumption
- Downtime costs

## 13. Vendor Management

### 13.1 Performance Reviews
- Monthly scorecards for strategic suppliers
- Quarterly business reviews
- Annual supplier audits
- Continuous improvement programs

### 13.2 Supplier Development
- Joint cost reduction initiatives
- Quality improvement programs
- Technology collaboration
- Capacity planning support

### 13.3 Relationship Tiers
- **Strategic**: Long-term partnerships, joint development
- **Preferred**: Consistent performers, growth opportunities
- **Approved**: Meet minimum requirements
- **Conditional**: Under performance improvement plan

## 14. Innovation & R&D

### 14.1 New Material Evaluation
- Material testing and qualification process
- Pilot production runs
- Cost-benefit analysis
- Risk assessment

### 14.2 Supplier Innovation
- Early supplier involvement (ESI) in product development
- Joint R&D projects
- Technology roadmap sharing
- Intellectual property agreements

## 15. Documentation Requirements

### 15.1 Mandatory Documentation
- Material test certificates (MTC)
- Certificates of analysis (CoA)
- Compliance certifications
- Traceability documentation
- Inspection reports
- Shipping documents

## 16. Emergency Procurement

### 16.1 Expedited Process
- Pre-approved emergency suppliers
- Expedited delivery (2-5 days)
- Premium pricing acceptable (up to 25%)
- Post-procurement review mandatory

## 17. Exceptions & Waivers

### 17.1 Exception Process
- Engineering approval for material substitutions
- Quality approval for specification deviations
- COO approval for policy exceptions
- Time-limited exceptions with review
