# IT Hardware & Technology Procurement Policy

## 1. Overview
This policy governs the procurement of IT hardware, including laptops, desktops, servers, network equipment, and related technology infrastructure.

## 2. Supplier Selection Criteria

### 2.1 Mandatory Requirements
- **Certifications Required**:
  - ISO 9001 (Quality Management)
  - ISO 27001 (Information Security)
  - ISO 14001 (Environmental Management)
- **Minimum Quality Rating**: 4.5/5.0
- **Delivery Reliability**: Minimum 90%
- **Financial Stability**: Audited financials for past 3 years

### 2.2 Preferred Qualifications
- Industry leadership position (Gartner Magic Quadrant)
- Global support capabilities
- Local service centers
- Warranty and maintenance programs
- Sustainability certifications (EPEAT Gold, Energy Star)

## 3. Product Categories

### 3.1 Laptops & Desktops
- **Approved Brands**: Dell, HP, Lenovo, Apple
- **Minimum Specifications**:
  - Intel i5/AMD Ryzen 5 or higher
  - 8GB RAM minimum (16GB preferred)
  - 256GB SSD minimum
  - 3-year warranty
- **Refresh Cycle**: 3-4 years
- **Budget Range**: $800-$2,500 per unit

### 3.2 Servers
- **Approved Brands**: Dell EMC, HP Enterprise, Cisco, Lenovo
- **Requirements**:
  - Redundant power supplies
  - Hot-swappable components
  - 5-year warranty minimum
  - 24/7 support
- **Budget Range**: $5,000-$50,000 per unit

### 3.3 Network Equipment
- **Approved Brands**: Cisco, Juniper, Aruba, Fortinet
- **Requirements**:
  - Enterprise-grade security features
  - Scalability for future growth
  - Compatible with existing infrastructure
  - Lifetime warranty preferred
- **Budget Range**: $1,000-$100,000 per deployment

## 4. Procurement Process

### 4.1 Approval Workflow
- **Under $10,000**: IT Manager approval
- **$10,000-$50,000**: IT Director + Finance approval
- **$50,000-$250,000**: CIO + CFO approval
- **Over $250,000**: Executive Committee approval

### 4.2 Vendor Evaluation
1. Technical capability assessment (40%)
2. Pricing competitiveness (25%)
3. Support and warranty terms (20%)
4. Sustainability and ESG scores (15%)

### 4.3 Contract Terms
- **Payment Terms**: Net 30-45 days
- **Delivery**: 10-14 days for standard items
- **Warranty**: Minimum 3 years for laptops, 5 years for servers
- **SLA**: 4-hour response for critical issues

## 5. Risk Management

### 5.1 Supply Chain Risks
- **Regional Concentration**: No more than 60% from single region
- **Supplier Dependency**: Maximum 40% spend with single supplier
- **Component Shortages**: Maintain 90-day buffer stock for critical items

### 5.2 Technology Risks
- **End-of-Life**: Plan replacements 12 months before EOL
- **Security Vulnerabilities**: Quarterly security assessments
- **Compatibility**: Test all new equipment before deployment

## 6. Sustainability Requirements

### 6.1 Environmental Standards
- EPEAT Gold certification preferred
- Energy Star compliance mandatory
- Recyclable packaging required
- E-waste recycling program with vendor

### 6.2 ESG Scoring
- Minimum sustainability score: 8.0/10
- Carbon footprint reporting required
- Conflict-free minerals certification

## 7. Quality Assurance

### 7.1 Acceptance Testing
- 100% inspection for orders over $50,000
- Sample testing (10%) for smaller orders
- Performance benchmarking against specifications
- Burn-in testing for servers (48 hours minimum)

### 7.2 Performance Metrics
- **Defect Rate**: Maximum 2% acceptable
- **Mean Time Between Failures (MTBF)**: Minimum 50,000 hours
- **First-Time Fix Rate**: Minimum 85%

## 8. Data Security & Compliance

### 8.1 Data Protection
- Secure data wiping before disposal
- Encryption capabilities required
- TPM 2.0 chip mandatory for all devices
- BIOS/firmware security features

### 8.2 Compliance Requirements
- GDPR compliance for EU operations
- SOC 2 Type II for cloud-connected devices
- Industry-specific regulations (HIPAA, PCI-DSS as applicable)

## 9. Cost Optimization

### 9.1 Volume Discounts
- 5% discount for orders over $100,000
- 10% discount for orders over $500,000
- Annual contract rebates for committed spend

### 9.2 Total Cost of Ownership
- Include maintenance costs in evaluation
- Factor in energy consumption
- Consider resale/trade-in value
- Account for training and deployment costs

## 10. Vendor Management

### 10.1 Performance Reviews
- Quarterly business reviews for strategic suppliers
- Annual performance scorecards
- Continuous improvement initiatives
- Issue escalation procedures

### 10.2 Relationship Management
- Dedicated account manager required for spend >$500K
- Regular technology roadmap discussions
- Early access to new products
- Joint innovation programs

## 11. Emergency Procurement

### 11.1 Expedited Process
- Pre-approved suppliers for urgent needs
- 24-hour delivery for critical items
- Premium pricing acceptable (up to 15% over standard)
- Post-procurement review required

## 12. Disposal & Lifecycle Management

### 12.1 Asset Disposal
- Certified e-waste recycling partners only
- Data destruction certificates required
- Asset tracking through entire lifecycle
- Resale or donation of functional equipment

## 13. Innovation & Future Technology

### 13.1 Emerging Technologies
- Annual evaluation of new technologies
- Pilot programs for innovative solutions
- Collaboration with vendors on R&D
- Technology refresh roadmap (3-year horizon)

## 14. Documentation Requirements

### 14.1 Mandatory Documentation
- Technical specifications
- Warranty certificates
- Compliance certifications
- User manuals and training materials
- Maintenance and support contacts

## 15. Exceptions & Waivers

### 15.1 Exception Process
- Written justification required
- CIO approval for policy exceptions
- Time-limited exceptions (maximum 6 months)
- Documented risk assessment
