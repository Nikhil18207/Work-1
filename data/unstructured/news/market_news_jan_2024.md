**Impact Level:** HIGH  
**Affected Categories:** Rice Bran Oil, Palm Oil  
**Regions:** APAC, Global

**Key Points:**
- Export licenses now required for shipments >100 MT
- Processing time: 7-14 business days
- Additional documentation requirements
- Potential delays in Q1 2024 shipments

**Procurement Impact:**
- Lead times may increase by 10-14 days
- Price volatility expected (+5-8%)

**Recommended Actions:**

**Impact Level:** MEDIUM
