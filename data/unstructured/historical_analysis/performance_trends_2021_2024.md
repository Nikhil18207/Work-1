## 9. ROOT CAUSE ANALYSIS

### Why is APAC Concentration Increasing?

**Primary Drivers:**
1. **Price Advantage:** APAC $170/MT cheaper than EU
2. **Lead Time:** APAC 7 days faster (15 vs 22 days)
3. **Relationship:** Established suppliers, easy to work with
4. **Performance:** Quality and delivery improving
5. **Convenience:** Same time zone, language, culture

**Secondary Factors:**
6. **EU Capacity:** Limited availability from EU suppliers
7. **Americas Distance:** Longer lead times, higher freight
8. **Procurement Behavior:** Path of least resistance
9. **Budget Pressure:** Focus on cost savings
10. **Short-term Thinking:** Quarterly targets over long-term risk
