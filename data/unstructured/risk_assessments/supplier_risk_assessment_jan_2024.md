## RISK SCORING METHODOLOGY

**Risk Score = Probability × Impact × Exposure**

Where:
- Probability: 0-100% (likelihood of occurrence)
- Impact: 0-100 (severity of consequence)
- Exposure: 0-100 (% of spend affected)

**Risk Levels:**
- 90-100: CRITICAL (immediate action required)
- 70-89: HIGH (action required within 30 days)
- 50-69: MEDIUM (action required within 90 days)
- 30-49: LOW (monitor)
- 0-29: VERY LOW (no action needed)
